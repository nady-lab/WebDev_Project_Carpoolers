Meet John O'Reilly

Who We Are

Hi, I'm John O'Reilly, the proud owner of Wheels On Fire. Cars have been my passion for as long as I can remember. Some kids collected trading cards or comics but I collected memories of engines roaring, chrome shining, and the smell of petrol hanging in the air. Over time, what started as a fascination became a career, and what became a career eventually turned into a business I'm genuinely passionate about.

What We Do

At Wheels on Fire, it's not just about buying and selling cars, it's about celebrating them. I've spent years curating vehicles that range from classic gems with stories etched into every dent and scratch, to modern machines that deliver performance and style in equal meansure. Every car is chosen with caare, and every detail is inspected, because let's face it, I'm a perfectionist. (I’m probably harsher on spark plugs than I am on people.)

When you visit this site, I want you to feel like you’re stepping into my garage. Explore the collection, take a closer look at the details, and imagine yourself behind the wheel. If a car speaks to you, I’ll do my best to make sure it finds its way into your story.

Our Philosophy

I believe cars are more than just machines; they're companions, adventrues, and sometimes even works of art. Every person who walks through my doors (or clicks through my site) deserves to find a car that makes them smile the moment they turn the key.
